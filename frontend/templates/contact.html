Common Help Topics

How long does physical book shipping take?

Physical Bookstore purchases are printed and packed on demand. Domestic shipping takes 3-5 business days. International delivery ranges between 7-14 business days, depending on customs clearance. You will receive a tracked carrier link via email as soon as your package leaves our facilities.

How can I publish my own novel on Novella?

We are currently in a curated closed beta for creators. If you are a serialized web writer or published author wishing to use our premium reader formats, check the "Author Dashboard" tab under your profile, or send a submission ticket to our editorial team using the contact form on this page.

Refund policy for digital and eBook purchases

Due to the immediate access nature of digital materials, we do not offer refunds on unlocked chapters or downloaded eBooks (EPUB/PDF format). However, if you purchased a physical hardback book that arrives damaged, please send a picture of the item to our support form, and we will issue a free replacement immediately.
